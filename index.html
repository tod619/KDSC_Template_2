About

KDSC Services Ltd was formed in June 2011 by Kevin Cummins & Lisa Byrnes. Kevin is a Chartered Quantity Surveyor with many years experience in both Ireland and abroad. Kevin has worked within the specialist MEP Services sector on many projects, from small commercial & retail developments to large scale projects including Data Centres and Pharmaceutical projects.

Lisa has an engineering background along with Production Management within the Medical Devise sector prior to moving into Health & Safety consultancy and training services in recent years. Her experience throughout the country ranges from small ad-hoc construction projects to working with multi-national businesses. The company was set up at the latter stages of the Economic recession in Ireland and has held its own and slowly grown each year. Our dedicated people are committed to only a handful of contracting firms each year so a fully dedicated service is always guaranteed
