Consultancy

With our Training partners we can help ensure your business or company are compliant. We can work with your team to ensure all activities under its control are as safe as reasonably practicable

  • Contract Safety Officers (Monthly, Quarterly and 6 Months) Holistic Control

  • Fire Safety Audits and Risk Assessme

  • DSE Assessment and Pregnant Employee Assessments

  • Occupational Monitoring and Hygiene Plans

  • Facilitation of In-House completion of risk assessments using Health and Safety Authority Guidance Documents

  • Development of action plans following risk assessments

  • Completion of Health and Safety Audits

  • Development of ongoing plans to help you comply with health and safety requirements
